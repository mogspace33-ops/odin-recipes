Stuffed Peppers

Stuffed peppers are in the weeknight dinner hall of fame, and for good reason. Strong enough to hold their shape, bell peppers are also large enough to hold a decent amount of filling while taking to a variety of flavors—they're the perfect vessel for countless combinations and ingredients. This versatile meal is not only simple to make, but feeds families big and small, making it a cheap & easy weeknight dinner legend. While I love to see all our experiments with countless variations, this classic, straight-forward recipe is hard to beat. Make it as-is using all my tips and tricks, then use it as a jumping-off point to get creative and make create your own dream stuffed peppers.

Ingredients

  • 2 Tbsp. extra-virgin olive oil, plus more for drizzling
  • 1 medium yellow onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, finely chopped
  • 2 Tbsp. tomato paste
  • 1 lb. ground beef
  • 1 1/2 cups cooked white or brown rice
  • 1 (14.5-oz.) can diced tomatoes
  • 1 1/2 tsp. dried oregano
  • Kosher salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • 6 bell peppers, tops and cores removed
  • 1 cup shredded Monterey jack
  • Chopped fresh parsley, for serving

Directions

  1. Step 3

    2. Arrange a rack in center of oven; preheat to 400°. In a large skillet over medium heat, heat oil. Cook onion, stirring occasionally, until softened, about 7 minutes. Stir in garlic and tomato paste and cook, stirring, until fragrant, about 1 minute more. Add ground beef and cook, breaking up meat with a wooden spoon, until no longer pink, about 6 minutes.

  2. Step 3

    3. Stir in rice and diced tomatoes; season with oregano, salt, and pepper. Let simmer, stirring occasionally, until liquid has reduced slightly, about 5 minutes.

  3. Step 3

    4. Arrange peppers cut side up in a 13" x 9" baking dish and drizzle with oil. Spoon beef mixture into each pepper. Cover baking dish with foil.

  4. Step 3

    5. Bake peppers until tender, about 35 minutes. Uncover, sprinkle with cheese, and continue to bake until cheese is bubbly, about 10 minutes more.

  5. Step 3

    6. Top with parsley before serving.
